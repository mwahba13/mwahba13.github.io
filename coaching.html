Common questions

Who is this for?

Anyone who wants to make games or work in the industry: beginners, hobbyists, students building portfolios, and career switchers making the jump.

What happens in the free intro consult?

We talk through where you are and what you want to build. No pitch, no sales pressure. At the end I'll share my honest take on how I can help, or whether you'd be better served by something else.

Do I need to know Unreal already?

No. I work with people across the full experience range. Tell me where you are and we'll go from there.

Single session vs. package?

Single sessions are for focused, one-off problems. The package is for bigger goals that take sustained effort: finishing a prototype, building a portfolio from scratch, getting job-ready.

How do sessions work?

Video call. You'll get a booking confirmation with a link after you reserve. Come prepared with whatever you're working on.
