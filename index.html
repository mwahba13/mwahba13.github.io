Michael Wahba

Who Am I?

That question cannot be answered with a single word. I am a writer, programmer, biologist, and designer. I have worn many hats and my interests are as diverse as my skillset.

Developer

I am a computer science student about to graduate from the University of Calgary. Outside of building strong fundamentals in data structures, algorithms and computation theory, I have directed my studies towards interactive digital media. My degree is concentrated in Computer Game Development which means I have also taken courses related to rendering, game design/development, and human-computer-interaction. It is my goal to push the limits of interactive media and show how it can be utilized in a variety of industries.

Writer

I have always enjoyed writing but never considered it as a career until recently. My first real foray into the field was coming in third place in Writerbyte.com's sci-fi short story contest in 2019. As a result, I have decided to pursue an embedded certificate in creative writing at the University of Calgary. Since then, some of my accomplishments include acquiring a position as lead writer on an upcoming indie game and publishing a poem in a printed anthology.

Scientist

More than anything, I am a curious mind. I want to learn more about how we can use technology to tell stories in new and exciting ways. I am formally trained in the scientific method having published peer-reviewed scientific papers and presented my findings to my colleagues. That collaborative method of pushing boundaries in a factual and efficient manner drives everything I do.
