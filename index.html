Michael Wahba

Who Am I?

That question cannot be answered with a single word. I am a writer, programmer, biologist, and designer. I have worn many hats and my interests are as diverse as my skillset.

Developer/Designer

My technical background lets me build the worlds to tell the stories I was born to tell. Right now, I am pursuing a Masters of Fine Arts in Interactive Media at the University of Southern California where I strive to learn from the best and improve my craft. I aim to push the limits of interactive media and show how it can be utilized across industries.

Writer

My first foray into writing was an award-winning submission in a short story contest for Writer Byte. After that I took as many writing courses as I could earning my Embedded Certificate in Creative Writing from the University of Calgary. Right now, I am working as the Lead Writer for Atrio: The Dark Wild and recently published a poem in YYC POP: Poetic Portraits of People

Scientist/Communicator/Teacher

My mind is a curious mind and I love to share that with others. I have applied the scientific method to solve problems and have published peer-reviewed papers. I have taught others and presented findings to groups of hundreds of peers. I want to learn how technology can enhance how we tell stories and I believe collaboration is how we will do that.
