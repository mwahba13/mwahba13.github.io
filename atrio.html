Atrio: The Dark Wild

A survival-automation game about finding hope in the darkness

About the Project

Atrio: The Dark Wild is a unique blend of survival, automation, and narrative storytelling. Set in a mysterious world where light itself seems to be fading, players must build automated systems to survive while uncovering the truth about their world and its inhabitants.

My Role

As Lead Writer, I was responsible for:

  • Creating the game's overall narrative and lore
  • Writing character dialogues and environmental storytelling
  • Developing the branching narrative system
  • Collaborating with designers to integrate story with gameplay

Narrative Design

  • Environmental storytelling through world design
  • Integrated narrative progression with automation mechanics
  • Dynamic dialogue system based on player choices

Project Details

  • Role: Lead Writer
  • Duration: 2023-2024
