The Immersive Archive

Preserving XR history through interactive recreation

About the Project

The Immersive Archive is a research and development project dedicated to recreating and preserving seminal XR experiences from the early days of the medium. Just as a film archive preserves classic cinema, the Immersive Archive aims to ensure that landmark XR works — many of which no longer run on modern hardware — remain accessible to researchers, educators, and future creators.

Started at the Mobile and Environmental Media Lab at USC, the project combines faithful recreation with scholarly documentation to build a living record of XR history.
