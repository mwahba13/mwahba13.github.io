About Me

Writer, designer, and software engineer working across indie games, triple-A studios, and academia.

I build across disciplines. At Maxis (Electronic Arts) I engineer the developer toolchain for The Sims. As designer and director on Egregore I led a 20-person team through USC's Advanced Games Program. As Lead Writer on Atrio: The Dark Wild I designed and wrote the narrative, lore, and dialogue for a commercially shipped Steam release. As XR Technical Lead on The Immersive Archive, the project won Best in Show at AWE 2024.

A background in synthetic biology, computer science, and creative writing gives me an unusual angle: I think in systems, write with precision, and design for the humans using what I build.
